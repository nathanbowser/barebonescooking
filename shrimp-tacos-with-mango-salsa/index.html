Shrimp Tacos with Mango Salsa

02 June 2015

It is Taco Tuesday at the Bowser’s and shrimp is on the menu tonight! We love tacos in all shapes, flavors, and forms and have eaten our fair share. I like to switch it up, and use seafood and shrimp in tacos, instead of always pork or beef. The shrimp come out a little spicy and very flavorful, with an added zing from some lime zest. The mango salsa is sweet and also a little spicy from the jalapeno. I like to buy the mangoes when they are on sale and leave them on my counter for a few days to ripen. Then I get to decide what to make with them! The best is just to eat them super ripe and juicy. I like the added crunch of the slaw and the creamy consistency is a nice balance to the taco. Make these for your next taco night!

Shrimp

  • 1 lb peeled and deveined shrimp
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • lime zest from 1 lime
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • corn or flour tortillas

Salsa

  • 1 mango - diced
  • 1 jalapeno - diced fine
  • ¼ red onion - diced fine
  • 1 tbsp cilantro
  • ¼ red pepper - chopped
  • the juice of 1 lime

Slaw

  • ¼ head cabbage - shredded
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • the juice of 1 lime
  • handful chopped cilantro

For the salsa: Mix ingredients and let the flavors mingle. Set aside.

For the Slaw: Mix the ingredients and let the flavors mingle. Set aside.

For the shrimp : Make sure shrimp are peeled and deveined. Lets be honest, I love it when they are available that way in the store, and even more when they are on sale, but that is not the norm. Set aside some time to prep the shrimp. Make sure shrimp are rinsed, drained, and pat them dry. Add the remaining ingredients from the list (cumin-salt) to the shrimp. At this point, you can let these marinate for a bit, or you can get ready to cook them up. They do not need long to marinate.

You have some options. You can pan fry the shrimp in 1-2 tsp of olive oil, which is what I did. You can also throw the shrimp on skewers and grill them, if the weather permits. I would prefer to grill the shrimp to get a nice char, but it has been raining all day. They are delicious either way!

To serve, add a few shrimp to each tortilla and top with slaw and salsa. Dig in!

Kristina Bowser
