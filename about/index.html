About

Me

Hey there, my name is Kristina, but most of my friends call me "Bones." I live in Philadelphia with my husband Nate and our hilarious boxer pup, Linus.

My story

As far back as I can remember, I've always loved to cook and bake. I am very fortunate to have grown up in a family where my mom cooked dinner nearly every night during the week. I remember always being in the kitchen with her, helping and learning. On top of this, my grandmother has been a huge inspiration for me in the kitchen. She and I are a lot alike and I owe a great deal of my cooking talents to her.

Why the blog?

I wanted a place to share my creations, since so often my family and and friends ask me, "what are you making tonight, Kristina?" I love sharing my food with my friends and family and truly enjoy having them over for dinner. I cook seasonally, looking forward to the fresh produce each new season has to offer. The recipes you will find here will be "mostly" healthy meals of all different cuisines. Some inspired by my Lebanese and Italian background, and others by my love of food and trying new things.

Are you ready to get cooking?
