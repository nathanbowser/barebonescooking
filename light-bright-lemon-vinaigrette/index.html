Light & Bright Lemon Vinaigrette

27 April 2015

Hey Everyone! I am back from my vacation hiatus and ready to post some light and healthy recipes. I think as soon as spring hits, we all have this mind-set to eat better, grill more, and lighten our food choices. Ironically, today is the first really nice day that feels like spring. The weather has been a real bummer lately, so what better way to “think spring” than a yummy and healthy salad.

This vibrant dressing is a great all around dressing to use for salads. The lemon is bright, the garlic adds a nice bite, and the honey sweetens the whole thing up. It is a really nice, flavorful balance. I used mine on spinach greens with tomatoes and scallions, but you can use yours on any combo.

  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ½ cup olive oil (I used a little less than this)
  • 1 clove of garlic, grated
  • 1 teaspoon of honey, can use more if you want it sweeter
  • a pinch of salt and pepper, to taste

This is such a simple vinaigrette to whip up and it is great on any salad. Just whisk all the ingredients in a small bowl and dress greens. As for the actual salad, use whats fresh, use what you’ve got, and mix up what you like!

Do you see that grilled bread up there? I brushed my baguette slices with the dressing and grilled it. It tastes like a tangy garlic bread. It really is the perfect compliment to the salad.

This could also be used as a marinade for chicken, fresh fish, and shrimp!

Kristina Bowser
